<p><b>Cowlersley</b> is a district 2 miles (3.2&#160;km) west of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. It is situated between <a href="/wiki/Milnsbridge" title="Milnsbridge">Milnsbridge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Linthwaite" title="Linthwaite">Linthwaite</a> and south of the <a href="/wiki/A62_road" title="A62 road">A62</a> and the <a href="/wiki/River_Colne,_West_Yorkshire" title="River Colne, West Yorkshire">River Colne</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The name Cowlersley means 'a charcoal burner's wood' and was first recorded in 1226.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The district has a primary school which was inspected by <a href="/wiki/Ofsted" title="Ofsted">Ofsted</a> in 2013 and listed as "requiring improvement".<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Its claim to fame is that the former <a href="/wiki/British_prime_minister" class="mw-redirect" title="British prime minister">British prime minister</a> <a href="/wiki/Harold_Wilson" title="Harold Wilson">Harold Wilson</a> was born and lived in Cowlersley in his early childhood,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> at 4 Warneford Road.
</p>

<p><b>Wilberlee</b> is a hamlet in the <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a> District, in the English county of <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>. It is near the town of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> and the village of <a href="/wiki/Slaithwaite" title="Slaithwaite">Slaithwaite</a>. Wilberlee has a school.
</p>

<p>The <b>International Association for Cryptologic Research</b> (<b>IACR</b>) is a non-profit scientific organization whose purpose is to further research in <a href="/wiki/Cryptology" class="mw-redirect" title="Cryptology">cryptology</a> and related fields. The IACR was organized at the initiative of <a href="/wiki/David_Chaum" title="David Chaum">David Chaum</a> at the CRYPTO '82 conference.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Activities">Activities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=International_Association_for_Cryptologic_Research&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Activities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The IACR organizes three annual flagship conferences, four annual workshops in specific sub-areas, and one symposium: <sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Crypto_(conference)" class="mw-redirect" title="Crypto (conference)">Crypto</a> (flagship)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Eurocrypt" title="Eurocrypt">Eurocrypt</a> (flagship)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Asiacrypt" title="Asiacrypt">Asiacrypt</a> (flagship)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Fast_Software_Encryption" title="Fast Software Encryption">Fast Software Encryption</a> (FSE)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/PKC_(conference)" class="mw-redirect" title="PKC (conference)">Public Key Cryptography</a> (PKC)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Workshop_on_Cryptographic_Hardware_and_Embedded_Systems" title="Workshop on Cryptographic Hardware and Embedded Systems">Cryptographic Hardware and Embedded Systems</a> (CHES)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Theory_of_Cryptography_Conference" title="Theory of Cryptography Conference">Theory of Cryptography</a> (TCC)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Real_World_Crypto" title="Real World Crypto">Real World Crypto</a> Symposium (RWC)</li></ul>
<p>Several other conferences and workshops are held in cooperation with the IACR. Starting in 2015, selected summer schools will be officially sponsored by the IACR. CRYPTO '83 was the first conference officially sponsored by the IACR.
</p><p>The IACR publishes the <i><a href="/wiki/Journal_of_Cryptology" title="Journal of Cryptology">Journal of Cryptology</a></i>, in addition to the proceedings of its conference and workshops. The IACR also maintains the <a href="/wiki/Cryptology_ePrint_Archive" title="Cryptology ePrint Archive">Cryptology ePrint Archive</a>, an online repository of cryptologic research papers aimed at providing rapid dissemination of results. <sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

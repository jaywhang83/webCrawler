<p><b>Richard Andrew Beaumont</b> (born 5 June 1961, <a href="/wiki/St_Albans" title="St Albans">St Albans</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hertfordshire" title="Hertfordshire">Hertfordshire</a>, England)<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> is a British <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> known for playing the role of Tiny Tim in the 1970 film <i><a href="/wiki/Scrooge_(1970_film)" title="Scrooge (1970 film)">Scrooge</a></i>, a musical version of <i><a href="/wiki/A_Christmas_Carol" title="A Christmas Carol">A Christmas Carol</a></i> by <a href="/wiki/Charles_Dickens" title="Charles Dickens">Charles Dickens</a>, starring <a href="/wiki/Albert_Finney" title="Albert Finney">Albert Finney</a>.
</p>

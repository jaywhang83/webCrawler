<p><b>Eorl Crabtree</b> (born 2 October 1982) is an English former professional <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league" title="Rugby league">rugby league</a> footballer who played in the 2000s and 2010s. He played at representative level for <a href="/wiki/England_national_rugby_league_team" title="England national rugby league team">England</a>, and at club level in the <a href="/wiki/Super_League" title="Super League">Super League</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Giants" title="Huddersfield Giants">Huddersfield Giants</a>, where he spent his entire club career, primarily as a <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#prop_forward" title="Rugby league positions">prop</a>, but also as a <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#Centre" title="Rugby league positions">centre</a>,&#32;<a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#Second-row_forward" title="Rugby league positions">second-row</a> or <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#Loose_forward_/_Lock_forward" title="Rugby league positions">loose forward</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Statistics_at_loverugbyleague.com_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Statistics_at_loverugbyleague.com-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Statistics_at_rugbyleagueproject.org_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Statistics_at_rugbyleagueproject.org-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-England_Statistics_at_englandrl.co.uk_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-England_Statistics_at_englandrl.co.uk-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eorl_Crabtree&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Cleethorpes" title="Cleethorpes">Cleethorpes</a>, Lincolnshire, Crabtree has lived in <a href="/wiki/Meltham" title="Meltham">Meltham</a> since he was three years old and attended <a href="/wiki/Honley_High_School" title="Honley High School">Honley High School</a>. He is the nephew of legendary professional wrestler <a href="/wiki/Shirley_Crabtree" title="Shirley Crabtree">Shirley "Big Daddy" Crabtree</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> himself a former professional <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league" title="Rugby league">rugby league</a> footballer who played for <a href="/wiki/Bradford_Bulls" title="Bradford Bulls">Bradford Northern</a>, although he never made an appearance for the first team.
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:77px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Eorl_Crabtree.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c5/Eorl_Crabtree.jpg/75px-Eorl_Crabtree.jpg" decoding="async" width="75" height="169" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c5/Eorl_Crabtree.jpg/113px-Eorl_Crabtree.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c5/Eorl_Crabtree.jpg/150px-Eorl_Crabtree.jpg 2x" data-file-width="256" data-file-height="576" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Eorl_Crabtree.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Crabtree while playing for Huddersfield in 2010</div></div></div>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Playing_career">Playing career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eorl_Crabtree&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Playing career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eorl_Crabtree&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Crabtree signed for Huddersfield on his 17th birthday and he made his début for the club in 2001. The following season he scored 25 tries in 37 league and cup appearances whilst playing at <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#Centre" title="Rugby league positions">centre</a> during Huddersfield's <a href="/wiki/Rugby_League_National_Leagues" class="mw-redirect" title="Rugby League National Leagues">National League</a> promotion season. His breakthrough year in Super League was 2004 where, under coach <a href="/wiki/Jon_Sharp" title="Jon Sharp">Jon Sharp</a>, he established himself as a regular in Huddersfield's first team and developed a reputation as an imposing figure with his mix of size and athleticism. Crabtree played for Huddersfield in the <a href="/wiki/2006_Challenge_Cup" title="2006 Challenge Cup">2006 Challenge Cup</a> Final as a <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#Second-row_forward" title="Rugby league positions">second-row</a> against <a href="/wiki/St_Helens_R.F.C." title="St Helens R.F.C.">St. Helens</a> but the Giants lost 12-42. Crabtree had a superb 2009 campaign at <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Giants" title="Huddersfield Giants">Huddersfield</a>. Having helped them to a third-place finish in the <a href="/wiki/Super_League" title="Super League">Super League</a>, and to <a href="/wiki/Wembley_Stadium" title="Wembley Stadium">Wembley</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Challenge_Cup" title="Challenge Cup">Challenge Cup</a> final, Crabtree also impressed national coach <a href="/wiki/Tony_Smith_(rugby_league_b._1967)" class="mw-redirect" title="Tony Smith (rugby league b. 1967)">Tony Smith</a> who selected Eorl in the <a href="/wiki/England_national_rugby_league_team" title="England national rugby league team">England</a> squad for the <a href="/wiki/2009_Four_Nations" class="mw-redirect" title="2009 Four Nations">2009 Four Nations</a>. At the end of the year, Crabtree signed a new long-term contract until the end of the 2015 season.
</p><p>Crabtree was an integral part of the Huddersfield team that finished top of <a href="/wiki/Super_League_XVIII" title="Super League XVIII">Super League XVIII</a>, and was one of four Huddersfield players selected in the <a href="/wiki/Super_League_Dream_Team" title="Super League Dream Team">Super League Dream Team</a> that year. In April 2014, Crabtree made his 350th appearance for the Giants in a 33-14 win over Warrington in <a href="/wiki/Super_League_XIX" title="Super League XIX">Super League XIX</a>. With this appearance he is currently the 14th most capped Huddersfield club player ever.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> In 2015, he signed an extension to his contract taking him until the end of the 2017 season.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> On September 3, 2015, Crabtree made history and became the 10th most capped club player of the Huddersfield Giants. He also scored a rare try on his 400th appearance for the club.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Crabtree announced his retirement as a player on 7 November 2016 to become an ambassador for the Giants.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He also makes regular appearances as a commentator and pundit for <a href="/wiki/BBC_Sport" title="BBC Sport">BBC Sport</a>'s rugby league coverage.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eorl_Crabtree&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Crabtree played 13 times at international level for <a href="/wiki/England_national_rugby_league_team" title="England national rugby league team">England</a>. He featured in the <a href="/wiki/2009_Four_Nations" class="mw-redirect" title="2009 Four Nations">2009</a> and <a href="/wiki/2010_Rugby_League_Four_Nations" title="2010 Rugby League Four Nations">2010</a> Four Nations tournaments, but missed out on selection in 2011.
</p>

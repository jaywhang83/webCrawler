<p>The <b>Rugby League Heritage Centre</b> was formerly located in the basement of the <a href="/wiki/George_Hotel,_Huddersfield" title="George Hotel, Huddersfield">George Hotel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England. It was the only rugby league heritage museum and was the brainchild of <a href="/wiki/Sky_Sports" title="Sky Sports">Sky Sports</a> presenter and former <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain_national_rugby_league_team" title="Great Britain national rugby league team">Great Britain</a> international <a href="/wiki/Mike_Stephenson" title="Mike Stephenson">Mike Stephenson</a>. 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rugby_League_Heritage_Centre&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Plaque_on_the_George_Hotel,_Huddersfield_(10252321744).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/67/Plaque_on_the_George_Hotel%2C_Huddersfield_%2810252321744%29.jpg/220px-Plaque_on_the_George_Hotel%2C_Huddersfield_%2810252321744%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="169" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/67/Plaque_on_the_George_Hotel%2C_Huddersfield_%2810252321744%29.jpg/330px-Plaque_on_the_George_Hotel%2C_Huddersfield_%2810252321744%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/67/Plaque_on_the_George_Hotel%2C_Huddersfield_%2810252321744%29.jpg/440px-Plaque_on_the_George_Hotel%2C_Huddersfield_%2810252321744%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2904" data-file-height="2235" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Plaque_on_the_George_Hotel,_Huddersfield_(10252321744).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Commemorative plaque to the foundation of Rugby League on George Hotel, Huddersfield</div></div></div>
<p>Twenty-one northern clubs held a meeting and by a majority of 20 to 1 voted to <a href="/wiki/History_of_rugby_league" title="History of rugby league">secede</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Rugby_Football_Union" title="Rugby Football Union">Rugby Football Union</a> to set up their own <a href="/wiki/Rugby_Football_League" title="Rugby Football League">Northern Rugby Football Union</a> at the George Hotel, Huddersfield on August 29, 1895. In 1922 this became the <a href="/wiki/Rugby_Football_League" title="Rugby Football League">Rugby Football League</a>.
</p><p>The Rugby League Heritage Centre was opened at the George Hotel on 30 August 2005 by former players <a href="/wiki/Billy_Boston" title="Billy Boston">Billy Boston</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neil_Fox_(rugby_league)" title="Neil Fox (rugby league)">Neil Fox</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mick_Sullivan" title="Mick Sullivan">Mick Sullivan</a>. The centre featured displays of memorabilia, including rare jerseys, medals, caps, programmes and photographs owned by <a href="/wiki/Mike_Stephenson" title="Mike Stephenson">Mike Stephenson</a>. 
</p><p>In 2013, the hotel was sold. The sporting charity, Rugby League Cares, was given £97,200 from the Heritage Lottery Fund to secure the collection. The collection was put on tour and in storage until a new location could be arranged.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2016, plans were announced for a new <a href="/wiki/National_Rugby_League_Museum" title="National Rugby League Museum">National Rugby League Museum</a>, based in nearby <a href="/wiki/Bradford" title="Bradford">Bradford</a>, to succeed the Heritage Centre.<sup id="cite_ref-RFL200916_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-RFL200916-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

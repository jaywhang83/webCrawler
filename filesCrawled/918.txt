<p>The <b>CB postcode area</b>, also known as the <b>Cambridge postcode area</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> is a group of sixteen postcode districts  in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, which are subdivisions of five <a href="/wiki/Post_town" title="Post town">post towns</a>. These postcode districts cover much of south and east <a href="/wiki/Cambridgeshire" title="Cambridgeshire">Cambridgeshire</a> (including <a href="/wiki/Cambridge" title="Cambridge">Cambridge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ely,_Cambridgeshire" title="Ely, Cambridgeshire">Ely</a>), plus parts of west <a href="/wiki/Suffolk" title="Suffolk">Suffolk</a> (including <a href="/wiki/Newmarket,_Suffolk" title="Newmarket, Suffolk">Newmarket</a> and <a href="/wiki/Haverhill,_Suffolk" title="Haverhill, Suffolk">Haverhill</a>) and north-west <a href="/wiki/Essex" title="Essex">Essex</a> (including <a href="/wiki/Saffron_Walden" title="Saffron Walden">Saffron Walden</a>).
</p><p>The districts CB21 to CB25 were created in September 2006 from parts of the districts CB1 to CB5.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

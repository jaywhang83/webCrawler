<p><b>Storthes Hall</b> is a part of the township of <a href="/wiki/Kirkburton" title="Kirkburton">Kirkburton</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England. A heavily wooded area, it comprises a single road, Storthes Hall Lane, which links Kirkburton with the nearby villages of <a href="/wiki/Farnley_Tyas" title="Farnley Tyas">Farnley Tyas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Thurstonland" title="Thurstonland">Thurstonland</a>. The most significant properties in the area are Storthes Hall Mansion (now a private property), <a href="/wiki/Storthes_Hall_Hospital" title="Storthes Hall Hospital">Storthes Hall Hospital</a> (located further west with the main administrative block surviving as a derelict building) and, finally, Storthes Hall Park Student Village which has been built on the old hospital site.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Storthes_Hall&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Mansion">The Mansion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Storthes_Hall&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: The Mansion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Storthes Hall Mansion was built as a private house for the mill owning Horsefall family in about 1788.<sup id="cite_ref-nhl_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nhl-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It is located close to Kirkburton centre and was renamed The Mansion Hospital when it became an independently managed facility for people with learning disabilities.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> After the Mansion Hospital closed in 1991, the building, which is <a href="/wiki/Grade_II" class="mw-redirect" title="Grade II">Grade II listed</a>, was converted back for private residential use.<sup id="cite_ref-nhl_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nhl-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Storthes_Hall_Hospital">Storthes Hall Hospital</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Storthes_Hall&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Storthes Hall Hospital">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Storthes_Hall_Hospital" title="Storthes Hall Hospital">Storthes Hall Hospital</a></div>
<p>An area to the west of The Mansion, closer to <a href="/wiki/Farnley_Tyas" title="Farnley Tyas">Farnley Tyas</a>, was developed as <a href="/wiki/Storthes_Hall_Hospital" title="Storthes Hall Hospital">Storthes Hall Hospital</a> in the early 20th century.<sup id="cite_ref-county_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-county-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> After the Storthes Hall Hospital closed in 1992, part of the Storthes Hall Hospital site was used as a training facility for <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Town_A.F.C." title="Huddersfield Town A.F.C.">Huddersfield Town A.F.C.</a><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> before their move to their state of the art Canalside facility off Leeds Road in 2011.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Storthes_Hall_Park_Student_Village">Storthes Hall Park Student Village</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Storthes_Hall&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Storthes Hall Park Student Village">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Much of the area previously occupied by the Storthes Hall Hospital was developed as a student <a href="/wiki/Campus" title="Campus">campus</a>, the Storthes Hall Park Student Village, for the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Huddersfield" title="University of Huddersfield">University of Huddersfield</a> in the mid 1990s.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> The village accommodates the largest single concentration of students from the University with over 1,300 students staying every year.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

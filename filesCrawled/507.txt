<p><b>Netherton</b> is a village, near <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a> <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_borough" title="Metropolitan borough">metropolitan borough</a> of <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England. It is 2&#160;miles (3&#160;km) south-west of the town centre on the road to <a href="/wiki/Meltham" title="Meltham">Meltham</a>. Netherton together with South Crosland have a population of 3,702 according to the 2001 census.
</p><p>It is part of the <a href="/wiki/Crosland_Moor" title="Crosland Moor">Crosland Moor</a> and Netherton ward of the <a href="/wiki/Colne_Valley_(UK_Parliament_constituency)" title="Colne Valley (UK Parliament constituency)">Colne Valley</a> parliamentary constituency. 
It has a junior school and an infant school.
</p><p>It also has a football club who play at South Crosland Junior school and Hawkroyd bank. They were also the founder members of the Huddersfield &amp; District Junior football league.
</p>

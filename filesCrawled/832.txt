<p><b>Jermaine McGillvary</b> is an <a href="/wiki/English_people" title="English people">English</a> professional <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league" title="Rugby league">rugby league</a> footballer who plays on the <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#Wing" title="Rugby league positions">wing</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Giants" title="Huddersfield Giants">Huddersfield Giants</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Super_League" title="Super League">Super League</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/England_Knights" title="England Knights">England Knights</a> and <a href="/wiki/England_national_rugby_league_team" title="England national rugby league team">England</a> at international level.<sup id="cite_ref-Profile_at_loverugbyleague.com_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Profile_at_loverugbyleague.com-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Statistics_at_rugbyleagueproject.org_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Statistics_at_rugbyleagueproject.org-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>He has spent time on loan from <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Giants" title="Huddersfield Giants">Huddersfield</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Batley_Bulldogs" title="Batley Bulldogs">Batley Bulldogs</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Barrow_Raiders" title="Barrow Raiders">Barrow Raiders</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jermaine_McGillvary&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>McGillvary was born in <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England, and he is of <a href="/wiki/Grenada" title="Grenada">Grenadian</a> descent.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Jermaine is a product of the Giants' academy system.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Playing_career">Playing career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jermaine_McGillvary&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Playing career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After signing for the <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Giants" title="Huddersfield Giants">Huddersfield Giants</a>, McGillvary was sent out on loan to <a href="/wiki/Batley_Bulldogs" title="Batley Bulldogs">Batley Bulldogs</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2009_Co-operative_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2009 Co-operative Championship">2009 Co-operative Championship</a>, a season which saw him progress as a talent and earned him selection in the 2009 Championship team of the season.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Batley_Bulldogs" title="Batley Bulldogs">Batley</a>/<a href="/wiki/Batley_Bulldogs" title="Batley Bulldogs">Batley Bulldogs</a>' "most tries in a match" record of 5-<a href="/wiki/Try_(rugby)" title="Try (rugby)">tries</a>, is jointly held by; <a href="/w/index.php?title=Joe_Oakland&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Joe Oakland (page does not exist)">Joe Oakland</a> (1908), <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Brannan" title="Thomas Brannan">Tommy 'Dowdy' Brannan</a> (against <a href="/wiki/Swinton_Lions" title="Swinton Lions">Swinton</a> at <a href="/wiki/Mount_Pleasant,_Batley" title="Mount Pleasant, Batley">Mount Pleasant</a>, <a href="/wiki/Batley" title="Batley">Batley</a> on Saturday 17 January 1920), <a href="/w/index.php?title=Jim_Wale&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Jim Wale (page does not exist)">Jim Wale</a> (1926 and 1927), <a href="/w/index.php?title=Tommy_Oldroyd&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Tommy Oldroyd (page does not exist)">Tommy Oldroyd</a> (against <a href="/wiki/Liverpool_Stanley#Highfield_–_1991–1996" title="Liverpool Stanley">Highfield</a> on 6 March 1994), <a href="/w/index.php?title=Ben_Feehan&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Ben Feehan (page does not exist)">Ben Feehan</a> (against <a href="/wiki/Halifax_R.L.F.C." title="Halifax R.L.F.C.">Halifax</a> on 18 August 2008), and Jermaine McGillvary (against <a href="/wiki/Whitehaven_R.L.F.C." title="Whitehaven R.L.F.C.">Whitehaven</a> on 24 May 2009).
</p><p>He made his first-grade début in 2010, starting on the right wing against <a href="/wiki/Bradford_Bulls" title="Bradford Bulls">Bradford Bulls</a> in round 16 of 2010's <a href="/wiki/Super_League_XV" title="Super League XV">Super League XV</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Huddersfield_Giants_Profile_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Huddersfield_Giants_Profile-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2011 McGillvary was awarded the Albert Goldthorpe Rookie of the Year Medal.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 4 May 2014 Jermaine made his 100th appearance for <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Giants" title="Huddersfield Giants">Huddersfield</a>. Fittingly he scored a try in his team's crucial 1 point win over 3rd placed <a href="/wiki/Super_League" title="Super League">Super League</a> team, the <a href="/wiki/Castleford_Tigers" title="Castleford Tigers">Castleford Tigers</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>McGillvary finished as Super League's top try scorer in 2015, with 27, and earned selection in the <a href="/wiki/Super_League_Dream_Team" title="Super League Dream Team">Super League Dream Team</a>. McGillvary's was selected in Steve McNamara's 24-man <a href="/wiki/England_national_rugby_league_team" title="England national rugby league team">England team</a> for their <a href="/wiki/2015_New_Zealand_rugby_league_tour_of_Great_Britain" class="mw-redirect" title="2015 New Zealand rugby league tour of Great Britain">test series against New Zealand</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> He made his début for England in the decisive final test-match at the DW Stadium.
</p><p>The following year, McGillvary was selected in the England squad for the <a href="/wiki/2016_Rugby_League_Four_Nations" title="2016 Rugby League Four Nations">2016 Four Nations</a>. Before the tournament, England played a <a href="/wiki/2016_Rugby_League_Four_Nations#France_vs_England" title="2016 Rugby League Four Nations">test match</a> against France in which McGillvary scored a try in England's 40-6 win.
</p><p>Following the 2017 season, McGillvary was named in Wayne Bennett's England squad for the <a href="/wiki/Rugby_League_World_Cup" title="Rugby League World Cup">World Cup</a> in Australia.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>McGillvary played on the wing in England's 6-0 defeat by Australia in the <a href="/wiki/2017_Rugby_League_World_Cup" title="2017 Rugby League World Cup">2017 Rugby League World Cup</a> final.  He finished the tournament with seven tries in five games and ran more meters than any other player in the tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

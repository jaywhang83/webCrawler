<p>The <b>A62</b> is a major <a href="/wiki/Road" title="Road">road</a> in <a href="/wiki/Northern_England" title="Northern England">Northern England</a> that runs between the two major cities of <a href="/wiki/Manchester" title="Manchester">Manchester</a> and <a href="/wiki/Leeds" title="Leeds">Leeds</a>, covering a distance of 38.5 miles (62.0&#160;km).
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Route">Route</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=A62_road&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Route">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The A62 runs north east from the <a href="/wiki/Manchester_Inner_Ring_Road" title="Manchester Inner Ring Road">Manchester Inner Ring Road</a> (beginning as <a href="/wiki/Oldham_Street" title="Oldham Street">Oldham Street</a> and Oldham Road) through <a href="/wiki/Failsworth" title="Failsworth">Failsworth</a> and <a href="/wiki/Oldham" title="Oldham">Oldham</a> then <a href="/wiki/Saddleworth" title="Saddleworth">Saddleworth</a> before crossing the <a href="/wiki/Pennines" title="Pennines">Pennines</a> at <a href="/wiki/Standedge" title="Standedge">Standedge</a> into <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>. Continuing through <a href="/wiki/Marsden,_West_Yorkshire" title="Marsden, West Yorkshire">Marsden</a>, <a href="/wiki/Slaithwaite" title="Slaithwaite">Slaithwaite</a>, <a href="/wiki/Linthwaite" title="Linthwaite">Linthwaite</a>, <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/Liversedge" title="Liversedge">Liversedge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Heckmondwike" title="Heckmondwike">Heckmondwike</a> and <a href="/wiki/Birstall,_West_Yorkshire" title="Birstall, West Yorkshire">Birstall</a>. It terminates just short of Leeds city centre at its junction with the <a href="/wiki/A58_road" title="A58 road">A58 road</a>. It has primary status from Manchester as far as Oldham.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=A62_road&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It is paralleled by part of the <a href="/wiki/M62_motorway" title="M62 motorway">M62</a> Trans-Pennine motorway. Prior to the building of the M62, the A62 was the main trans-Pennine road between Oldham and Huddersfield. The M62 is numbered after the A62 and now takes most of the long-distance traffic between Manchester and Leeds.
</p>
